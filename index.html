A podcast about dating that discusses sex, dating and relationships from both women's and men's perspective.

What do women and men want? A question we all have and all think we have the answers to. Whether you think you have the answer or not, Darmante & guests will have open, honest, and fun conversations about it all.

About

My name is Darmante and im the host of What Wo(men) Want! I'm originally from Pittsburgh, PA but I now reside in Brooklyn, NY. The topic of dating is something I constantly speak about with friends, family, and even strangers. With a wealth of first hand experience and countless stories/scenarios from others, I decided why not take these great conversations to a platform for the world to tune into. My goal is to cultivate a community that shares and learns about dating, sex, and relationships. Be sure to tune in!

Follow our Instagram page to give your input and engage with the "Questions of the Day" that are asked on the story. Below are just a few that keep everyone talking!

  • Do you think you should share a bank account with your S/O?
  • Can you recycle nicknames you had for your Ex to your new S/O?
  • What's the most toxic thing you've done after a breakup?
  • What makes dating you so difficult?
  • Are you ok with your parter being friends with your ex?
